I am a 5th year undergraduate student in the Electrical Engineering Department at Sharif University of Technology. Also, I am a remote research assistant at L3S Laboratory, Hanover, Germany, where we're studying the explainability of Vision Language Models.

My main research interest lies in applying large foundation models to different tasks such as robotics, computer vision, etc. I am also interested in Graph neural Networks, especially dynamic GNNs, for analyzing temporal networks.
